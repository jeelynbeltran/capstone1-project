Music Classes

The students will learn to appreciate music as an art through various activities involving movements, action songs, dancing and musical games. Musical concepts like high and low, fast and slow, loud and soft, etc. will be taught and the students will also develop skills such as identifying colors/shapes, cutting, painting, and number/alphabet recognition. The students will acquire a repertoire of songs and will be expected to be able to echo-back melodies in tune and repeat simple rhythms.
